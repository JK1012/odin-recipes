Squash Surprise (The Grey Method)

Ingredients

  • Summer Squash
  • Kidney Beans
  • Brown Rice
  • Adobo Seasoning

Steps

  1. Sautee summer squash over high heat until soggy
  2. Add in cooked brown rice (wet) and kidney beans
  3. Cook until a slurry is formed. Consistency should be of grey goop
  4. Add in adobo seasoning until edible
