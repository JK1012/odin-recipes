Easy No-Knead Dutch Over Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Cups Warm Water
  • 1 Packet (2.25 tsp) Active Dry Yeast
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 3.25 cups All Purpose Flour

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Cover with a towel and let rise for 2 hours
  3. Put dough into parchment paper-lined dutch oven
  4. Bake in covered dutch oven for 45 min at 450 deg F
